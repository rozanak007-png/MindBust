Frequently Asked Questions

Is MindBurst completely free?
Yes, 100%. All tools on MindBurst — the quiz, spin wheel, mood tracker, and tips — are completely free with no sign-up required.
Are my quiz answers stored or shared?
No. Your quiz responses are processed entirely in your browser and are never sent to our servers. We do not store any of your health data.
Is the depression quiz a real clinical tool?
Our quiz is based on the PHQ-9, which is the standard clinical depression screening tool. However, our version is for informational use only and is not a clinical diagnosis.
Can I use MindBurst instead of seeing a therapist?
No. MindBurst is a wellness support tool, not a replacement for professional care. If you are struggling, please reach out to a licensed mental health professional.
How do I report a bug or technical issue?
Use the contact form above and select "Report a Bug". Please describe what happened, what browser you are using, and what you expected to happen.
Can I share MindBurst with others?
Absolutely! We encourage you to share MindBurst with anyone who might benefit. The more people we can help, the better.
